# 📊 E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard (Multi-Year Analysis)

This project consists of two interactive Tableau dashboards designed to provide a comprehensive view of business performance across **Sales** and **Customer** dimensions.

The dashboards support dynamic **3-year comparison** using interactive filters, enabling flexible period-over-period analysis.

Design Structure:
Executive Summary → Diagnostic Insights → Deep Dive Analysis

---

# 🏢 Dashboard 1: Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🎯 Objective
Provide leadership with a high-level and diagnostic view of revenue, profitability, and product-level performance across multiple years.

The dashboard enables users to dynamically select any year and analyze:

- Year-over-Year growth
- Seasonal patterns
- Category-level contribution
- Profitability trends

---

## 📌 Executive KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity

Each KPI includes:
- YoY % comparison
- Monthly sparkline trends
- Highest and lowest month indicators
- Dynamic updates based on selected year

---

## 📊 Analytical Views

### 1️⃣ Sales & Profit by Subcategory
- Multi-year comparison (Selected Year vs Previous Year)
- Profit vs Loss contribution highlighting
- Identification of high-revenue but low-margin categories
- Subcategory performance benchmarking

### 2️⃣ Sales & Profit Trends Over Time
- Periodic sales tracking
- Average reference lines for benchmarking
- Above/Below average performance visualization
- Profit fluctuation analysis
- Seasonal spike identification

---

## 🔍 Sales Insights Enabled

- Revenue growth trend tracking across years
- Margin compression detection
- Identification of underperforming categories
- Detection of seasonal demand spikes
- Contribution analysis using subcategory breakdown

---

# 👥 Dashboard 2: Customer Performance Dashboard

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze customer growth, monetization efficiency, and purchasing behavior across multiple years.

The dynamic year filter enables:

- Cross-year customer growth comparison
- Sales per customer trend tracking
- Order volume evolution
- High-value customer contribution analysis

---

## 📌 Executive KPIs

- Total Customers
- Sales Per Customer
- Total Orders

Each KPI includes:
- YoY growth %
- Monthly trend visualization
- Highest and lowest month indicators
- Dynamic year-based filtering

---

## 📊 Analytical Views

### 1️⃣ Customer Distribution by Number of Orders
- Purchase frequency segmentation
- Identification of one-time vs repeat buyers
- Long-tail high-frequency customer detection
- Behavioral distribution analysis

### 2️⃣ Top 10 Customers by Profit
- Ranked customer contribution analysis
- Profit vs Sales comparison
- Order count evaluation
- Last order tracking for recency insights

---

# 🧠 Dashboard Design Framework

Both dashboards follow a 3-layer analytical structure:

1️⃣ Executive Summary Layer  
High-level KPIs for leadership review  

2️⃣ Diagnostic Layer  
Category and customer behavior analysis  

3️⃣ Performance Monitoring Layer  
Trend evaluation, benchmarking, and distribution analysis  

Designed to support:
- Weekly Business Reviews
- Category Strategy Discussions
- Growth & Retention Planning
- Executive Reporting

---

# ⚙ Interactive Features

- Dynamic Year Filter (3-year comparison)
- Year-over-Year Growth Calculations
- Conditional highlighting (Highest / Lowest month)
- Reference lines for average benchmarking
- Cross-filter interactions
- Drill-down capability
